My Community

A Social Media platform for new people in town.

About the Project

My Community is a social media platform that aims to gather communities and help newcomers and immigrants to have an easy adaptation process.

The Challenge

Migrants from all around the world experiences the same problems.

  • Language Barriers

    Not feeling comfortable speaking in another language.

  • Housing

    Not being able to find a suitable place to stay.

  • Isolation

    Feeling lonely and not having anyone to talk to.

  • Paperwork and Legal Documents

    They feel lonely and can not find anyone to refuge.
